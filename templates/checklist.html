Checklist

Before you click or pay

Run through these items whenever a message feels rushed, emotional, or unusually demanding.

  • Is the sender verified through an official channel?
  • Is the request asking for OTP, PIN, password, card details, or screen sharing?
  • Does the link use a familiar domain and HTTPS?
  • Are you being rushed, threatened, or promised money?
  • Does the payment request match a known and trusted contact?
  • Can you confirm the request from the official app or website directly?
