Recovery

What to do after a scam attempt

Use this sequence to reduce impact, preserve evidence, and alert the right parties quickly.

Stop contact

Do not reply further, approve requests, or share more information.

Secure accounts

Change passwords, revoke sessions, and enable MFA on critical accounts.

Preserve evidence

Save screenshots, phone numbers, payment IDs, and message timestamps.

Report quickly

Use the incident report form and contact your bank, wallet provider, or platform support.
